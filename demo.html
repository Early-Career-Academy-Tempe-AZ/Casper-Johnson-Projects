Back Story

In a land, known only to myth and legend, the world has three guardians, the Guardians of Light.

The Sky, vast, stretching higher and farther than any creature could travel.
The Sun, bright and blazing, providing the warmth of life to the creatures of the land, and burning away the shadows.
The Moon, providing balance, the benign night to the sun's day, the pull and push of the waves, acting as an equal and opposite force.
